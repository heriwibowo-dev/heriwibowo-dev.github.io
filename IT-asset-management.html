was made for IT asset management, to enable IT departments to track who has which laptop, when it was purchased, which software licenses and accessories are available, and so on.

built on Laravel, it follows a standard Laravel MVC file structure. Within the IT Asset management project, you'll see a public directory. That directory should be set as your document root.

Challenge

All assets are recorded and monitored in one organized system.

  • Centralised Asset Management :
    All IT assets (laptops, printers, licenses, etc.) are managed in one platform.
  • Accurate Asset Tracking :
    Easily track who has which asset, when it was assigned, and its current status.
  • Multi-user with Role Access :
    Assign roles (admin, staff, user) for secure and organized access.
  • Detailed Asset History :
    Keeps a full audit log (check-in/out, maintenance, transfers, etc.).

Solution & Result

1. Automates asset check-in/check-out, ownership history, and real-time status
2. Assets are assigned to specific users with timestamps and detailed records
3. Automatic email notifications for renewals, returns, or expiring warranties
4. Unified, real-time, and cloud-accessible asset database
5. Detailed logs for all asset activities: who did what and when
